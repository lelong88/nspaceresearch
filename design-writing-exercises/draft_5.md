## Bài tập 5B – Viết đoạn văn phản hồi cá nhân (Personal Response – 8-10 câu)

Chọn **một** câu hỏi. Sử dụng ít nhất **10 từ vựng**.

### Câu hỏi 1:
> After reading this article, has your view on health and aging changed? What is one thing you want to do differently starting today?

### Câu hỏi 2:
> Think about the healthcare system in your country. Is it closer to Medicine 2.0 or Medicine 3.0? What would need to change to improve people's healthspan?

### Câu hỏi 3:
> Imagine yourself at age 80. What does your ideal "marginal decade" look like? What are you doing to make that vision possible now?
