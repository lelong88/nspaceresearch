Target audience: Vietnamese children aged 6-10
Total: 22 curriculums, 1 collection, 3 series

## Overview

22 German-learning curriculums for Vietnamese children aged 6-10, organized into:
- **6 beginner mini** (1 session, 5 words, price 9) — Series 1
- **8 beginner short** (4 sessions, 10 words, price 19) — Series 2
- **8 preintermediate short** (4 sessions, 12 words, price 49) — Series 3

All marketing text (title, description, preview) in Vietnamese targeting parents.
All learner-facing content: Vietnamese with German words + pronunciation guidance + Vietnamese meaning.
Reading passages: entirely in German.

### Verify series membership and display orders

```sql
SELECT cs.title as series_title, cs.display_order as series_order,
       c.id as curriculum_id, c.content->>'title' as curriculum_title,
       c.display_order, c.price, c.language, c.user_language
FROM curriculum_series cs
JOIN curriculum_series_items csi ON cs.id = csi.curriculum_series_id
JOIN curriculum c ON csi.curriculum_id = c.id
WHERE cs.title IN ('Bước Đầu Tiên', 'Xây Nền Vững Chắc', 'Khám Phá Thêm')
ORDER BY cs.display_order, c.display_order;
```

FROM curriculum_collection cc
