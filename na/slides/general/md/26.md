# Manulife Agency Center (MAC) | Market comparison

![Source](../images/26.jpg)

## Key Points

- Pru is mostcomprehensive cover wide range of performance metrics. Compared to that, MAC only not cover RYP bonus due to premiums collection carried by Company, this can be combined with P13 modifiers or bonus
- Franchise will have growth bonus as there is no target after first 2 years setup, under MAC this already taken care of by SM target and also target achievement modifiers in compensation
- Most of them office allowance is paid in fix amount (not variable) because in GA compensation, variable production already included. Total variable bonus is ~15% - 20%. Chubb have the highest because of 20% 2nd year RYP
- In term of targets: Pru targeting bigger size (6-10bil) compared to Chubb & Gen (from 3-4bil to 6-8bil) . Pru recruit up meaning selection criteria is lower than first year target. Chubb has a 6 months probation periods which require they to produce ~400mil/mth.
- Maintenance validation: Gen after 2 years, Chubb has assessment every 6 months with 50% target achievement and P13 >~50%
