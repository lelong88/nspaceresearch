## MAC (Manulife Agency Center)

- MAC (Manulife Agency Center) is a small sales offices set up by SM+ in untapped/underserved location distant from MVL branch, in exchange they are compensated with a combination of fixed and variable bonus that tied to their production levels and office costs are partially subsidized by MVL:
  - This leverages sales leaders' entrepreneurial mindset while also provided a higher financial security for them as opposed to fully franchise-model.
  - This helps solving existing challenges from Branch model by shifting office operation to sales managers with some company oversights through partial costs subsidy mechanism.
- We have implemented this one with step-by-step approach to minimize execution risks, hence first to pilot recruitment in Apr'25 and to review the scheme for on-going implementation approval.
