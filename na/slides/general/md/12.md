**Key bullets:**
- For existing 46 branch offices, our sales is generated with **66% FYP PREs** and approximately **~20% FYP** for office operation costs (exclude. CAPEX) in last 2 years and estimated to be at **~16% FYP for 2025**.
- For 20 MAC offices, we'd have the same expenses for **PREs** but instead of spending on fixed costs for set-up, operate and maintain the office ourselves, we will subsidize SM partially with performance-tied office allowance at **~13% FYP for first 3 years and 8% FYP onwards** and shift office operation burden to sales managers with some company oversights
