## We DO

- Use clear, everyday language instead of technical terms
- Explain how insurance supports long-term financial security
- Clarify how benefits work and what customers should expect
- Distinguish guaranteed vs. non-guaranteed elements
- Show how insurance fits within a broader financial plan

*Red X icon*

## We do NOT

- Product structure or benefits
- Pricing, fund mechanics, or projections
- Operations, claims, or system processes
