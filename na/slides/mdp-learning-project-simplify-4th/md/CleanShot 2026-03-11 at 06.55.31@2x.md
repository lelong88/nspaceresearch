## Step 2: Online Game Show

### Game Show: Confetti Format

**Fun | Engaging | Interactive | Knowledge Sharing**

Format: Livestream → 15 multiple-choice questions → real-time answers
When: 1 round/month — Fuel-up Friday, 1:30 – 2:00 PM
Where: FB Livestream "Manulife Tốt hơn mỗi ngày"
Target: Internal staff (Phase 1), then Sales force (Phase 2)

### Game Flow

*Flow diagram with 5 steps connected by arrows, each with a colored icon:*

1. Topics Announced → 2. Materials Shared → 3. Livestream Starts → 4. 15 MCQ Questions → 5. Winners Announced

### Design Principles

**Collaboration over Competition**
Participants can discuss answers with each other — embrace knowledge sharing, not rivalry

**Pre-shared Materials**
Summary/keynote shared before each round — easy to review

**Phase 1: Product Features**
Start with product knowledge for internal staff

**Phase 2: Cross-functional**
Expand to all MVL functions: Operations, Distribution, Legal & Compliance, CX, Campaigns
