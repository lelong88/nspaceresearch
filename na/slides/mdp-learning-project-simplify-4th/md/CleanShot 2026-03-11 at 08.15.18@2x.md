# Evaluation Questions

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-03-11 at 08.15.18@2x.png)

Here are the questions the EMT and your colleagues will use to evaluate your presentation:

- How effectively did the team articulate their solution, define their deliverables, and outline the steps they will take to execute the plan?
- Did the team realistically identify and optimize people, technology, and budget requirements to deliver the solution?
- To what degree does the team's implementation approach actively provide outcomes that is Stronger, Faster, Better for customers and/or internal users?
- How credible and measurable are the expected benefits (e.g., ROI, savings, revenue uplift, efficiency gains)?
