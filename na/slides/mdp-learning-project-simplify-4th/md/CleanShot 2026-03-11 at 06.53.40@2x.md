# CORE PROBLEM: Complexity exists on two layers

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-03-11 at 06.53.40@2x.png)

## 1. The first layer is the complexity of the product structure

| LEGAL LOGIC (Technical information) | CUSTOMER LOGIC (real reading flow) |
|---|---|
| • Definitions | • What do I get? |
| • Terms | • When will I get paid? |
| • Benefits | • In which cases am I not covered? |
| • Exclusions | |
| • Appendices | |
| • Illustrations | |

*Blue arrow pointing right →* **This mismatch forces customers to "translate" the content themselves**

## 2. The second layer is the complexity in communication

- Advisor training focuses on *sales skills* and *revenue KPIs*
- Clarity is *not measured*, so it never becomes a system-wide priority
- Limited consultation time leads to conditions and exclusions being *skimmed over*

*Green arrow pointing right →* **Customers receive incomplete or oversimplified explanations**
