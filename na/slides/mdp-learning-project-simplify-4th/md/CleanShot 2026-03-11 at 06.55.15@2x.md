## 😊 Why This Matters

- Staff don't need to memorize 36 slides. They experience the product through their own profile — age, budget, family situation — and see exactly how Xanh Tương Lai works for a real customer.

- The interactive demo turns passive reading into active exploration. Staff can play with scenarios ("what if the customer dies in year 3?", "what happens in a market crash?") and build genuine product intuition.
