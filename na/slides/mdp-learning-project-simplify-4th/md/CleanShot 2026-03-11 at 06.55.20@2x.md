# Insurance Payout Simulator: Interactive vs. Raw Slides

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-03-11 at 06.55.20@2x.png)

**DEMO #2 — BENEFIT SIMULATOR**

## ❌ Traditional: 36-Page Illustration Document

*Screenshot of a traditional Manulife illustration document titled "XANH TƯƠNG LAI (PHIÊN BẢN PHÍ ỔN ĐỊNH)" — a dense spreadsheet with many rows and columns of numbers*

- 36 pages of tables, numbers, legal disclaimers
- Fixed to one customer profile — can't explore "what if"
- Staff struggle to explain payout mechanics
- Customers confused by wall of numbers

## ✅ Interactive Payout Simulator

*Screenshot of an interactive simulator titled "Mô Phỏng Chi Trả Bảo Hiểm" — Sản phẩm Xanh Tương Lai — Manulife Việt Nam, showing a green banner "Câu chuyện bảo hiểm của bạn" with interactive icons for different life scenarios: Tử vong năm thứ 3, Tử vong năm thứ 15, Bệnh hiểm nghèo năm thứ 30, Duy trì đến năm thứ 30, Hợp đồng mất hiệu lực*

- Adjust age, premium, fund — see results instantly
- Visual story: "what happens if you die in year 3?"
- Year-by-year account value projection chart
- Staff can demo live to customers in meetings

## 😟 Why This Matters

- A 38-page illustration document is a compliance artifact, not a learning tool.
- Staff can't internalize payout mechanics by reading tables — they need to play with the numbers.
- The simulator lets staff input any customer profile and instantly see how benefits work across scenarios.
- It builds confidence for real sales conversations and replaces rote memorization with hands-on understanding.
