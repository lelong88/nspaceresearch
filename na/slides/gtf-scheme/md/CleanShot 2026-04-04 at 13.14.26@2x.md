# D. Annual High Performing Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.26@2x.png)

**AHPB = Total Agency Management Income * AHPB Bonus Ratio**

| Whole Team APE Requirement (VND billions) | Total Agency Management Income Requirement (VND millions) | AHPB Bonus Ratio |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **0** | <350 | 0% |
| **1** | 350 to 450 | 3% |
| **1.5** | 450 to 550 | 6% |
| **2** | 550 to 750 | 9% |
| **3** | 750 to 950 | 12% |
| **4** | 950 to 1200 | 15% |
| **6** | 1200 to 1600 | 18% |
| **8** | 1600 to 2100 | 21% |
| **10** | 2100 to 4000 | 24% |
| **20** | 4000 to 10000 | 27% |
| **40** | Over 10000 | 29% |

- Annual High Performing Bonus was introduced in 2022, with a view to provide certain tax equalization measures for agency income.
- Payment Schedule: There are 2 payments:
  - First payment (proceeded in 1 month before Tax Finalization time): pay 50% of calculated bonus;
  - Second payment (proceeded after Tax Finalization): pay the rest 50% of calculated bonus.
- Those who leave Manulife at any point of time during the year shall not be eligible.
