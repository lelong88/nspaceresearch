## Maintenance of Contract

- Maintenance of Contract (MOC) assessment is conducted quarterly, with the first assessment done after the first 6 months from appointment

- GTF members under 18 months belongs to Group 1; over 18 months belongs to Group 2

- GTF members who do not meet the MOC requirements will be demoted. They can be promoted back to SM through the organic SM promotion criteria.

### 2. Headcount limit for existing PSM+

Provided that existing PSM+ satisfy the below criteria:

1. Existing PSM+ & his direct report achieve >= 80% contract maintenance criteria
2. #CA per PSM+ in his location must exceed company requirement
3. APE per PSM+ in last 12 months must exceed company requirement
4. Percent of unqualified PSM+ (achieve <80% contract maintenance criteria) must below company requirement
