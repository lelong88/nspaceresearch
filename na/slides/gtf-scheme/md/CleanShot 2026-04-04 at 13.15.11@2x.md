# Financial Assessment – Sensitivity testing

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.15.11@2x.png)

- Most of the SM compensation, regardless of whether he/she is an GTF SM or not, are variable income, meaning that the income as % of APE is quite stable.

- Manulife shall be slightly better-off with high sales leading to reduction of fixed payment, yet will be worse off in case of lower persistency (for GTF SM in this proposal the year-end bonus and clearance of sign-on bonus shall subject to persistency adjustment).

- We assume baseline APE contribution of VND197b from new GTF recruitment in 2023, with consideration given to ramp up period required for new SMs who are joining us and building their team from scratch.
