## Clearance and Claw-back

- The entire GTF Sign-on Bonus is paid upfront and is **subject to clearance and claw-back mechanism**.
- See Appendix on claw-back enforcement considerations.

### Bonus Clearance

| Year | Bonus Amount Cleared |
|------|---------------------|
| **1** | 40% |
| **2** | 30% |
| **3** | 30% |

- Must achieve Standard Annual APE Target of the year; AND
- Must have 19M persistency over 75% (starting in Y2).
- If prior year clearance criteria is not met, GTF member will have a chance to catch up in the following year as long as cumulative criteria are met. If current year is met but cumulative not met, then only current year amount is cleared.

### Remaining Uncleared Amounts

- If after the 3 year GTF program period, or if the GTF member is removed from the GTF program for any reasons whatsoever; and there is still any remaining uncleared Bonus Amount, such amount will become a debt of the GTF member with Manulife Vietnam.
- Such cases may arise where the GTF member continues to be with Manulife, but has not been able to reach sales performance targets.
- In such case, Manulife Vietnam will **deduct up to 10% of monthly income** from GTF member until the uncleared Bonus Amount is paid off.

### Upon Leaving Manulife

| Year | Claw-back % of Bonus Amount which is NOT CLEARED |
|------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **1** | 100% |
| **2** | 100% |
| **3** | 100% |
| **4+** | 100% |

- If for any reason the GTF member leaves Manulife (whether voluntary or involuntary), the claw-back mechanism will be activated.
- Any uncleared Bonus Amount will be clawed back by Manulife at 100%.
- Manulife shall, in such order, seek to:
  - Claw back directly from Agent.
  - Claw back from the immediate upline manager who recruited the Agent, up to the amount which they had benefited from the recruitment only.
  - Pursue legal recourse for debt collection.
