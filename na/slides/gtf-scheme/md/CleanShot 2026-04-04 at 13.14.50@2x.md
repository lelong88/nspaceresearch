# Addition of GTF Sign-on Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.50@2x.png)

**<u>Background</u>**

- Through our years of experience operating the GTF program, we have identified two pain points:
    - **<u>Debt Obligation Repayment:</u>** Some industry Agency Leaders have financial debt obligations to their existing Insurer. This means they will not be willing to join Manulife for fear of the need to satisfy a sum of debt with no guarantee that Manulife will provide them a better platform for income generation.
    - **<u>Loss of Prior Income:</u>** Some industry Agency Leaders have an entrenched policy portfolio with their existing Insurer, such that by moving to Manulife, they may stand to lose a material amount of renewal/continuous portfolio income.
- These two pain points primarily impact the high-performing and quality Agency Leaders/Groups which we would like to target.
- As a result of these two scenarios, we propose to add a GTF Sign-on Bonus element into the GTF scheme.

**<u>Eligibility Criteria</u>**

- GTF prospect will be required to show proof of portfolio persistency to us. See Appendix for example.
- Persistency threshold is set with reference to Manulife past 24 month persistency rates in Agency channel. See Appendix.
