# Organic Promotion to SM vs. Inorganic Recruitment of SM Through GTF

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.13.58@2x.png)

## Organic Promotion of SM

- We currently have in place an organic promotion program for SDM level or above to be promoted to SM.

- A high level summary of key promotion criteria as follows:

  - Last 6 months total APE above VND6b
  - 3MAA of at least 90
  - Last 3 months avg 1MAA of 35%
  - 3 or more UM+ direct reports
  - Balanced team requirement (i.e. no single source of production contributing over 60%)

- The criteria for organic promotion to SM is generally considered harder than GTF recruitment to SM. However, we will broadly benchmark for GTF SM to reach organic SM target levels by the end of the 3yr GTF program.

## GTF SM

- In order to (1) provide a strong recruitment proposition; and (2) provide sufficient time and support for inorganic GTF SM recruits to achieve scale, the following additional elements are offered to GTF Agency Managers, on top of the standard organic compensation scheme:

  - Higher Monthly Fixed Allowance
  - APE target is fixed based on GTF scheme schedule, rather than last year's performance
  - Provision of a guaranteed minimum level of income for first 36 months (subject to targets)
  - Year end bonus for initial 3 years
  - Sign-on bonus available to selected candidates

- These additional GTF components are available for a period of 3 years only, after which the GTF recruit will revert back to the normal agency manager schemes.
