## Monthly Fixed Allowance

| Grading | Amount (VND millions) |
|---------|----------------------|
| **SM** | 35 |
| **SSM** | 45 |
| **DRD** | 50 |
| **RD** | 60 |
| **SRD** | 80 |
| **AVP** | 110 |
| **SAVP** | 125 |
| **VP** | 150 |

## Multipliers

| % of SAAT | Multiplier |
|-----------|-----------|
| <30% | 0% |
| ≥ 30% and ≤ 60% | 50% |
| > 60% and ≤ 115% | 100% |
| > 115% | 120% |

- GTF members will receive a monthly fixed allowance based on grading, plus a multiplier based on achievement of Standard Annual APE Targets per grade and year.

- Monthly Fixed Allowance has been aligned to be the same between GTF scheme and Standard scheme since Sep 2022.
