# Commissions Fraud Assessment

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.15.17@2x.png)

- Usually the dummy risk analysis in compensation scheme is made on the field managers and agents only, as the SM compensation is smaller compared to the entire team. Yet in this analysis we assumed the entire structure from Agent to SM are constructed for dummy. The part for Agents and Managers are estimated using factor base basis from highest scenario of dummy risk analysis during 2023 Compensation Proposal (total post-tax is ~130% FYP).
- Generally the commissions fraud is highest at SM level for which the sign-on bonus will be highest as % of FYP, for AVP level 4.5 bil VND is not a major %.
- While the SM parts are recalculated for review scope of this proposal, the calculation suggests that the key driver comes from sign-on bonus at 25% FYP. However this is paid to settle SM's debt obligation to previous company and/or compensating for loss of income. There should be little to no net monetary gain for the agent from the sign-on bonus.
- The dummy risk scenarios is constructed as below:
  - SM is expected to meet 200% standard APE to achieve have sign-on bonus
  - Maximum number of layers SM to maximize indirect overriders in scenario of team C
  - All of the production under this SM is maximizing Agency Compensation scheme, meaning that all of them need to be FTA/FTM and MDRT+ (1,200 FYP/ year) at the same time, maximum layers for Field Managers. This is very difficult to construct.
