# E. Year End Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.29@2x.png)

*Blue highlighted banner:*

**Year End Bonus Amount = 5% * (Actual APE – Standard Annual APE Target)**

**Upper Limit Cap: 12x Base Figure of Monthly Fixed Allowance**

- Year End Bonus (YEB) is a monetary incentive for over-production beyond the Standard Annual APE Targets.
- YEB is applicable for all 3 years within the GTF scheme.
- In order to qualify for YEB, 19M Persistency Rate must be 75% or above.
    - This criteria is not applicable for Y1 YEB.
