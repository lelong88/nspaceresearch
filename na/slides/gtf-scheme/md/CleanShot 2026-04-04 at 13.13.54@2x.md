## GTF Strengths
- Fixed targets for 3yrs to provide certainty and incubation for growth
- Guaranteed Minimum Income element provides some peace of mind to entice joiners
- Tax equalization elements
- Year-end bonus for over-production

## GTF Weaknesses
- Lack of upfront incentives to attract the "big sharks" in the market (hence the new addition of GTF Sign-on Bonus)
- Year-end bonus only in Y1 (hence now extend to include all 3 years)
- Competitors have low Y1 targets (albeit hockey stick increase in later years)
