# Modification of Year End Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.53@2x.png)

- In the Sep 2022 approved version of the GTF scheme, a Year End Bonus was included for 1 year only, and with no upper limit cap.

- **We would like to modify this such that:**
  - **Year End Bonus is applicable across all 3 years of the GTF scheme, not only in Year 1**
  - **Add a upper limit cap on the Year End Bonus (to limit maximum payout)**

- Note that we propose to continue to use APE as basis for Year End Bonus calculation, given we have set the GTF scheme entirely based on Standard Annual APE Targets. Whilst there were suggestions to switch to FYP as basis for calculation, we feel this would introduce an additional layer of complexity in the conversions and calculations, whereas by introducing an upper limit cap we can already nevertheless reduce financial risk.

---

**Extension of Year End Bonus to 3 Years**

- The Year End Bonus is an overproduction incentive, so that Agency Managers will not only seek to reach the minimum SAAT, but to continue to produce beyond reaching the target.

- We would like to continue to incentivize and drive overproduction of Agency Managers in Y2 and Y3.

---

**Year End Bonus Upper Limit Cap**

- The current Year End Bonus has no upper limit cap, which means theoretically an Agency Manager can significantly overproduce and have unlimited upside potential. This poses some level of risk to Manulife.

- Two recent cases in particularly have raised some alarm bells, whereby they were able to significantly exceed SAAT during the first year.

- Hence we propose to add a Year End Bonus Upper Limit Cap at 12x Monthly Fixed Allowance.
