# Overview of Growth Task Force (GTF) Scheme

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.13.50@2x.png)

*Manulife Vietnam's proven industry recruitment scheme since 2016*

- We currently have 2 GTF Schemes in place – (1) GTF-SM+ for senior agency leaders; and (2) GTF-UM+ for entry level and field/sales managers. This document is focused on GTF-SM+ only.
- Manulife's GTF scheme was initially launched in 2016. It is focused on **insurance industry recruitment of experienced Agency Leaders**
  - Up until Sep 2022, the GTF scheme only provided 1 year of support to new GTF leaders. This posed a challenge for our business because 1 yr is often not enough time for new join GTF units to scale up. Further, sales targets for Y2 and Y3 were not fixed in advance, which created uncertainty for potential candidates who wanted to join.
  - In Sep 2022, the base GTF scheme was updated to become a 3 year scheme, inclusive of 3 years of fixed targets and associated benefits/incentives.
- GTF contribution is considered successful in the Vietnam BU, contributing around 35% of agency APE and total headcount.
