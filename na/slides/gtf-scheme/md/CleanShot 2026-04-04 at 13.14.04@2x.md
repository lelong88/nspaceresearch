# GTF Scheme Overview

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.04@2x.png)

- GTF Scheme is a 3 year program which acts as
    - (1) A Recruitment Proposition to entice industry agency leader to join Manulife; and
    - (2) To provide GTF recruits with initial support whilst they build up the foundations of their agency group within Manulife.
- After 3 years the GTF member will be reverted to the Standard Agency Manager compensation structure only.
- The GTF scheme components sits on top of (i.e. incremental to) the "Normal Agency manager compensation scheme" (Normal).
- There are 7 financial compensation components in the GTF Scheme:
    - A. Monthly Fixed Allowance (exists in both GTF and Normal)
    - B. Monthly Variable Income (exists in both GTF and Normal)
    - C. Quarterly Variable Income (exists in both GTF and Normal)
    - D. Annual High Performing Bonus (exists in both GTF and Normal)
    - E. Year End Bonus (GTF only)
    - F. Guaranteed Minimum Income (GTF only)
    - G. Sign-on Bonus (GTF only)
- Various of the above compensation components will be subject to standardized performance validation requirements, namely:
    - Standard Annual APE Target (SAAT)
    - 3 Month Active Agent Numbers (3MAA)
