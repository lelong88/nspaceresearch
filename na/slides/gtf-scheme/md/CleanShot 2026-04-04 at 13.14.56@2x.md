# Adjustment of Clawback Clause for Guaranteed Income and Year End Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.56@2x.png)

- In the Sep 2022 approval of the GTF scheme, a claw-back clause was included for terminated agents, as below.

- **<u>We would now like to remove this clause</u>**, for the following reasons:
    - The inclusion of this clause was largely an oversight in the submission, and we would like to take the immediate step to rectify
    - Claw-back upon termination should not apply to guaranteed income, otherwise the income is not guaranteed. To further reiterate, guaranteed income is an "income floor" only. Agents leaders who are able to meet guaranteed income stretch targets almost always already earn actual income higher than the guaranteed income floor.
    - Claw-back upon termination should not apply to year-end bonus, as the year-end bonus is an overproduction reward incentive designed to reward agency leaders who have exceeded Standard Annual APE Targets.

- **<u>HOWEVER</u>**, claw-back and immediate withholding of any further compensation payments will continue to be applicable for any cases where fraud or misconduct is involved.

> **Claw-back Clause**
>
> Apply claw-back for SM to receive guaranteed income and/or year-end bonus but leave/stop company before 36th month for any reasons. Details below:
>
> - Fixed allowance is not clawed back
> - Variable income and year bonus shall be clawed back as below
>     - 100% variable income and 1ˢᵗ year bonus if the new SM leaves before 24th month
>     - 50% variable income and 1ˢᵗ year bonus if the new SM leaves from 25th – 36th month
