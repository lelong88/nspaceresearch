## GTF Recruitment Workflow

*Flowchart with three green boxes connected by downward arrows:*

### Recommendation by Agency Leader

- DRD+ will find a GTF candidate, and provide the candidate profile and recommendation to HQ Recruitment team
- Same level agency leader cannot hire another same level leader or above.

↓

### Submissions Screening

- Candidate to provide all required documents to HQ Recruitment team, who will do screening.
- Some candidates may be rejected, some may be recommended for lower ranking.

↓

### Interviews

- Various levels within HQ Recruitment team will do interviews according to the candidate grading. DRD or below can be interviewed by Head of Dept; above DRD will require interview by CAO.
- Fill in interview evaluation form.

---

## Selection Requirements

- Candidates will be hired into <u>same or similar rank</u> as their previous company
- <u>Documents Required:</u> (1) Interview form; (2) Application form; (3) National ID; (4) Appointment letter / Agent Contract; (5) Bank Statements; (6) Sales results; (7) Certificates; (8) 1yr plan; (9) Evidence of resignation from prior company; (10) Others.
- <u>Key Skills for Interview Screening:</u> (1) Business mgt experience in financial services; (2) Team management experience; (3) Presentation and communication skills; (4) College education or above; (5) Market understanding and influence.
- <u>Some Red Flags:</u> (1) Already resigned or not having worked in insurance for more than 12 months; (2) Moving through a lot of insurers within a short period of time; (3) Not willing to share documentation or sales results.
- Quantitative Criteria:
