- Standard Annual APE Targets (SAAT) shall be reviewed annually and adjusted based on market conditions with approval from local leadership team.
- Current fixed targets have been increased 20-30% from prior scheme in 2022.
- Targets are set based on Agency Manager reaching Agency Channel median APE production by grading by end of Year 3. (see Appendix)
- If a GTF member is promoted during the 3yr GTF scheme period, his/her targets will be revised to the new grading level.
