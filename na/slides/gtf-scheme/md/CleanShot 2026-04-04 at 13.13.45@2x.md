## Industry recruitment of Agency Leaders continues to be a driver of sales growth

- As at Jan 2023, we estimate there are around 6000+ insurance agency leaders in the Vietnam life insurance market who are of the equivalent of Manulife SM grading or above.
    - Manulife currently has around 500 agency leaders at SM grade or above (i.e. Agency Leaders\*)
    - We aim to recruit c.120 additional SM+ agency leaders in 2023, with increasing focus towards the "big sharks" rather than entry level SM. (We expect c.10% of new GTF to be eligible for sign-on bonus scheme)
- In the past 5yrs, Manulife has cumulatively recruited over 380 GTF-SM+, who have subsequently recruited over 35% of our total agency recruitment in FY2022.
    - This type of recruitment has been considered highly successful due to the high performance, engagement, and retention rates of these agents.
    - GTF agents have contributed around 30-40% of Agency Channel APE in the last 4 years.
