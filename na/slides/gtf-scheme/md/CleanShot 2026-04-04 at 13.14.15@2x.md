- 3MAA validation requirements is used for Quarterly Variable Income component only.
- Definition of 3MAA is the number of in-force agents who has sold a minimum of one net case in the last rolling 3 months. Cases shall satisfy the minimum premium required for a life case, and excludes top-ups.
- Net case count is net of free look and lapse within first year.
