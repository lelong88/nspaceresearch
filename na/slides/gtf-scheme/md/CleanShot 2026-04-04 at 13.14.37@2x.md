# G. GTF Sign-on Bonus (1/2)

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.14.37@2x.png)

## Sign-on Bonus Eligibility Criteria

- Must have been with existing Insurer for a period of no less than 12 months; and should not have held any job for < 6 months in the past 3yrs without valid reasons.
- Must have <u>total income over VND1bn</u> within the last 12 months.
- Avg sales performance over last 12 months must be <u>at minimum 2x Standard Annual APE Target</u>
- Avg portfolio persistency must be either:
  - <u>13M Persistency Rate > 75% (preferred)</u>
  - <u>19M Persistency Rate > 80%</u>

- GTF prospect must show proof of portfolio persistency. See Appendix example.
- Persistency threshold is set with reference to Manulife past 24 month persistency rates in Agency channel.

## Bonus Amount

| Grading | Amount | Approval Limit |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **SM, SSM** | Up to VND 1.5 billion | CAO & CFO |
| **DRD, RD, SRD** | Up to VND 2.5 billion | CFO & CEO |
| **AVP, SAVP, VP** | Up to VND 4.5 billion | Regional Approval |

- The final amount shall be based on EITHER (but not both):
  1. Debt notification letter of prior Insurer
  2. Loss of earned income from prior Insurer
- **In any case, the total amount of the bonus shall be capped at 1x of the last 12 months total income of the candidate.**
- As a rule of thumb, sign-on bonus should be utilized for "big sharks" in the market, and amounts below VND1b should be exception not the norm.
- For FY2023, the estimated total local budget for sign-on bonuses is approximate VND15b, depending on total agency channel APE (translating to c.0.6% of FYC). We expect around 10% of GTF-SM+ to be on the Sign-on Bonus scheme.
