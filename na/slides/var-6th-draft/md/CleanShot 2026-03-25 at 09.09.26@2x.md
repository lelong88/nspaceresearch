## ⚠ Impact of new product ILP with forecast 50% FYP contribution

- New ILP product introduces a minimum premium that is 2.5 times higher than current case size (minimum of 50 mil vs. Agency's current average case size of only 20.3 mil as of Dec'25). This product is projected to contribute approximately 40% of total Agency FYP by year-end 2026.

- This increase overall average productivity FYP/active by +41% from 38.6 mil to 54.4 mil. These improvements are expected to elevate average productivity levels across the entire team. As of March 23rd, this new ILP26 (Ever Green) product has already contributed 87% of month-to-date FYP and increased actual case size to 71.1 mil.

- Given the compensation structure in which payout rates rise with higher productivity, these changes are forecasted to increase VaR from ~0.9 mil VND to ~2.5 mil VND. In addition, no. of units with positive VaR & positive production is expected to increase 2.6 times from 168 to 436.
