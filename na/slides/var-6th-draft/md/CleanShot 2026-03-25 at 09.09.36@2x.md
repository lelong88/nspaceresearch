**Escalation protocol when threshold is breached**

When monthly VAR exceeds threshold, local Compensation team and Line1B will provide a deep-dive analysis. Line1B already manages monthly protocols for other risk controls — this is integrated into existing workflows, not a new standalone process. Multi-indicator screening includes: persistency ratios, sale patterns, phone/bank sharing, payout anomalies.
