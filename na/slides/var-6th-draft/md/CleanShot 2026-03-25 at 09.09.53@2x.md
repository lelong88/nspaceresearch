# Approved VaR Methodology

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-03-25 at 09.09.53@2x.png)

*Formula banner with icons:*

> **VaR = Total Payout - Total FYP**

## Payout include

- ✅ First year commission + rider bonus
- ✅ Compensation, financing scheme, contests, benefits

## Exemption for payout

- ❌ CSV first year because currently CSV is only ~0.4% FYP for current product portfolios due to high allocation charge in first year
- ❌ PSM+ income is excluded in VaR data for this proposal but still be monitored by Local team on monthly basis to flag any significant fluctuation for further action discussion

---

*Three-step process flow connected by arrows, each with an icon above:*

### Rolling 12 months

*Icon: calendar/balance diagram*

- Total payout each month = ∑Sum of payout per month in last 12 months
- Basis of rolling last 12 months to:
  - ✓ Reflect full 12-month sales cycle
  - ✓ Mitigate impact of late payment post free-look

➡️

### Direct team

*Icon: org chart diagram*

- VaR Direct Team of UM
  = ∑Sum of payout in last 12 months
  - ∑FYP of all FA direct & UM
- Only count at month of rank UM+

➡️

### Identify VaR of Agency

*Icon: chart/graph diagram*

- Only count UM with VaR direct team L12M >0, zeroizes team with negative value
- VaR Agency = ∑Sum of all VaR direct team L12M >0
