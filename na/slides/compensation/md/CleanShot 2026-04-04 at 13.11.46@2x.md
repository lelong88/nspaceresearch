## Current Scheme

Overrides = Bonus rate * % current month FYC of direct reports

| Report level | Current month FYC of direct reports (level 1) | Bonus rate (% x FYC) |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 (direct reports) | ≥ 48 mil | 20% |
| | ≥ 24 mil | 16% |
| | ≥ 7 mil | 14% |
| | < 7 mil | 10% |
| Level 2,3,4 | ½ of prior level | |

- Higher levels will receive 50% of the override of the immediately preceding lower level, up to level 4.
- Direct report is included all FA and UM+ at reporting level 1
