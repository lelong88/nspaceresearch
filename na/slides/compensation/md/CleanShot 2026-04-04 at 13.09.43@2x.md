# UM+ Compensation | Expected payout

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.09.43@2x.png)

New UM+ compensation scheme places greater emphasis on rewarding Top Leaders (MBA) who excel in activating their agents and driving quality recruitment. As a result:
- MBA UM+ will experience a significant income increase due to higher bonus rates in Monthly Team Activation Bonus and New Agent Development Bonus, which also lead to a higher Retention Bonus.
- Increased bonus rate in MBA Pro will lead to a substantial income boost when UM+ become MBA Pro - encouraging non-MBA leader to run for the title by recruiting and developing new agents to become Manulife Pro

*Flow diagram showing progression:* **Non-MBA Pro** → **MBA Pro - Silver** → **MBA Pro - Gold** → **MBA Pro - Platinum**
