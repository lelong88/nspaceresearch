## 1. SM+ fix allowance

- Fix payout = Fix Amount by rank x % modifier; max 120% fix amount by rank

| Rank | PSM/ SM | PSSM/SSM | DRD/RD | SRD | AVP | SAVP | VP |
|------|---------|----------|--------|-----|-----|------|----|
| mil VND | 35 | 45 | 60 | 80 | 110 | 125 | 150 |

| % hit FYP target | <30% | ≥30% & <60% | ≥60% & <80% | ≥80% & <115% | ≥115% |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| % modifier | 0% | 50% | 80% | 100% | 120% |

**Proposed scheme:**
- Fix payout = Fix Amount by rank x % hit FYP target x 130%
- Max: 150% fix amount by rank
- Minimum requirement: % hit FYP target ≥ 30%
- **Fix amount by rank unchanged**

| % hit 1mAA target | ≥90% & <100% | ≥100% & <110% | ≥110% & <120% | ≥120% | x P13 modifier |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Direct PSM+ (% FYC) | 1.0% | 2.5% | 3.5% | 5.0% | |
| Indirect PSSM+ | 50% of direct report | | | | |

- *1mAA = min 1CC & 4mil FYC per month*
- *Total quarterly target = sum of monthly 1mAA target in the quarter*

**Proposed scheme:**
- Change P13 modifiers
- Active agent requirement includes SA active

## 4. Retention bonus

| Group | Bonus = Bonus rate * [Monthly + Quarterly Production Bonus in the year] |
|---|---|
| Direct location | 75% of [Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those direct location] |
| Indirect location | 25% of [Total Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those indirect locations] |

- **Eligibility:** ≥ 85% FYP target and P13 ≥ 65% **OR** TOP 15 SM/PSM/SSM/PSSM and P13 ≥ 65%
- **Payment in 3 years:** 30% - 30% - 40% with criteria **P13 ≥ 65%** and maintain PSM+ rank
- **Maximum:** 12 months of fix allowance (by rank at year-end)

**Proposed scheme:**
- Change P13 threshold from 65% to 70%
