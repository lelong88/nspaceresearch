# Risk Assessment and Mitigation

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.10.33@2x.png)

| Risk type | Description | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Strategy risk | Risk of seller's resistance to major changes in comp scheme | Low | Medium | • Prepare compensation launching plan to ensure effective communication including embed in kick-off sessions, details training materials, FAQ, income simulation tools... |
| Communication risk | | | | • Post-launch monitoring of agency performance and qualifiers against changes is done monthly to track effectiveness. |
| Operational risk | Risk that system cannot handle the changes in comp scheme | Low | Low | • We have worked with Corporate Payroll, Tax, IT, MIS, Agency Operation to align on system ready timelines and back-up plan for manual calculation with sufficient doer-checker mechanism to launch the new schemes. Please refer to appendix for system enhancement timeline |
| Financial risk | Risks that cost may deviate from the estimate due to changes in the sales pattern in the future, making history a bad proxy for future. | Low | Low | • Sensitivities suggests that the cost are still within manageable range in most scenarios. • **Monthly compensation payout** monitoring including Production Related Expense (PRE) is conducted monthly after the implementation of new scheme to ensure the design and changes are effective. Comparison of relevant ratios against prior periods or trends to trigger investigations and explanations for material deviation and for timely rebalance cost by reduction of contest. • The scheme is re-evaluated on a yearly basis through the **Annual Renumeration Review** (ARR) to assess the compensation and financing schemes are complied with the best practices set by Regional Compensation Playbook as well as the Arbitrage Risk within local risk appetite. |
