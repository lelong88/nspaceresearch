## Current Scheme

### 1) Promotion requirement:

| Title Manulife Pro | Requirement | | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| | **CC** | **FYP (mil)** | **# active month in last 12 months** | **P13** |
| Platinum L12M | 30 | 600 | 6 | 65% |
| Gold (> 12mths) L12M | 24 | 360 | 6 | 65% |
| Gold (≤ 12mths) L12M | 12 | 240 | 6 | 65% |
| Silver (L3M) | 4 | 60 | 3 | 65% |
| Silver fast track (M0-M2 agents) | 3 | 40 | - | - |

- *Active month: agent with ≥ 1CC & 4mn FYC in month*
- Agent will still be eligible to remain in the qualified tier for a maximum period of 12 months from the month of promotion
- *Not require P13 if agents not yet have P13*
- *For fast track require completed new agent training & attendance 80% in M1 &M2*

### 2) Maintenance MOC requirement
- After 12 months from Promotion, Agent will be evaluated based on MOC Requirements.
- If Agent cannot meet the Maintenance Criteria, then Agent's performance will be compared to Entry Criteria for lower tier and be allocated to the relevant Tier for the next 12 months.
- If an Agent is downgraded, they will not be considered for promotion to higher tiers again for a period of 3 months.

|---|---|---|---|

| Gold | 300 | 6 | 65% |
| Silver | 100 | 3 | 65% |

## Proposed Scheme

| Title Manulife Pro | CC | FYP | # active month | P13 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum L12M | 24 | 600 mil | 6 | 70% |
| Gold (tenure > 12mths) L12M | 15 | 360 mil | 6 | 70% |
| Gold (tenure ≤ 12mths) L12M | 10 | 240mil | 6 | 70% |
| Silver (tenure ≥ 3mths) L12M | 5 | 100 mil | 3 | 70% |
| Silver (tenure ≥ 3mths) L3M | 3 | 40 mil | 2 | 70% |
| Silver Fast Track (M0-M2) | 3 | 40mil | - | n/a |

- *Active month: agent with ≥ 1CC & 4mn FYC in month*
- Training & activities requirement for title classification will be dependent on local agent development roadmap and subject to local CAO approval
- Agent will still be eligible to remain in the title for a maximum period of 12 months from the month of promotion.
- After 12 months from Promotion, Agent will be evaluated again based on same Manulife Pro Criteria and be allocated to the relevant Tier for the next 12 months.
