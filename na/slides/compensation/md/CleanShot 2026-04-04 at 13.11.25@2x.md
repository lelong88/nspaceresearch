# Agent Compensation | Manulife Pro Agent Yearly Retention Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.25@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📋

- Adjust the #CC requirement to align with average current case size ~25mil-20mil/ policy
- Increase P13 requirement in payment year from 65% to 70%
- Keep RYP transition (higher amount between (10%-15% FYC) or (1.5% RYP of policies in last 10 years))

### Current Scheme

**6) Retention bonus**

Agent Retention bonus = Full year FYC * Bonus rate* P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Title Manulife Pro | CC | FYP | Bonus (% FYC) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum | 30 | 600 mil | 15% |
| Gold (> 12mths) | 24 | 360 mil | 10% |
| Gold (≤ 12mths) | 18 | 240 mil | 10% |

**Additional requirements:** 13mth persistency ≥ 65%

- *Maximum bonus:* **100mil**
- *Payment plan: paid in 3 times* in next 3 years: 50% - 30% - 20% if personal P13 ≥ 65%; waive if no personal P13, must still maintain agents or UM/PSM+ contracts.
- *DC modifier:* is applied for each payment schedule based on DC in Dec of production year
- *Manulife Pro title:* hold title at Dec or qualify for title at Dec assessment (based on production cutoff at Dec)
- *Adjustment for 202 top producer RYP bonus:* in case old RYP scheme > existing scheme, payout different amount (2022 top producer bonus scheme = [1.5% x RYP collected in the assessed year for policies within 10 nearest years])

### Proposed Scheme

**6) Retention bonus**

Agent Retention bonus = Full year FYC * Bonus rate* P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Title Manulife Pro | CC | FYP | Bonus (%FYC) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum | **24** | 600 mil | 15% |
| Gold (> 12mths) | **15** | 360 mil | 10% |
| Gold (≤ 12mths) | **10** | 240 mil | 10% |

**Additional requirements:** 13mth persistency ≥ **70%**

- *Maximum bonus:* **100mil**
- *Payment plan: paid in 3 times* in next 3 years: 50% - 30% - 20% if P13 ≥**70%**, waive if no P13, must still maintain agents or UM/PSM+ contracts.
- Apply new threshold P13 ≥**70%** to payments of Y3 payment of 2024 bonus; Y2&Y3 payment of 2025 bonus); *P13 modifier: at Dec of production year*
- *DC modifier:* is applied for each payment schedule based on DC in Dec of production year
- *Manulife Pro title:* hold title at Dec or qualify for title at Dec assessment (based on production cutoff at Dec)
- *Adjustment for 202 top producer RYP bonus:* in case old RYP scheme > existing scheme, payout different amount (2022 top producer bonus scheme = [1.5% x RYP collected in the assessed year for policies within 10 nearest years])
