**Key changes:**

---

*Icon: P13 modifier (AG, UM+, SM+)*

## Raise threshold: *apply from Q2'2026*

- ✓ Raise threshold to receive 100% payout: **Agents 65%** –>**70%**; **UM/SM 60%** –> **70%**
- ✓ Strengthen penalty for below min threshold: %modifier on payout = **actual P13 / 2** (current %modifier = actual P13)

---

*Icon: Agent Comp*

## Remains unchanged with minor adjustments

- ✓ New agent bonuses: allow for rehired agents ≥24 months
- ✓ Referral bonus: M0 active requirement –> M0 or M1 active
- ✓ M –star quarterly & yearly bonus: increase bonus tier to align MDRT requirement and room to adjust locally for each year
- ✓ Manulife Pro – *lower case count* to align with case size, encouraging more runners
- ✓ Manulife Pro title: *simplify by combining promotion & MOC title assessment*

---

*Icon: UM+ Comp*

## Increase investment in quality recruitment & developing core agents targeting Top leaders – MBA Pro

- ✓ **Monthly team activation bonus:** higher bonus rate & lower threshold to better reflect current manpower & encourage leaders in activating their agents
- ✓ **New Agent Develop bonus:** x2 bonus rate for top leaders (MBA Pro) to drive quality recruitment and New Manulife Pro Silver development
- ✓ **MBA Pro:** simplify assessment mechanism
    - ○ **Assessment:** moving from rolling 12-mth quarterly assessment –> Annual calendar assessment + minimum monthly requirement to receive MBA Pro higher rate – serving as a form of "MOC"
    - ○ **Retention bonus:** extend to MBA Pro Silver (currently only Gold & Platinum is eligible)
- ✓ Add activities requirement for compensation payout subject to Local approval

---

*Icon: PSM+ Comp*

## Break income comfort trap

*SM+ fix allowance:*

- ✓ Replace uniform tier-based payout –> payout proportional to %target achieve: ***Payout = % target achieve \* 130% \* fix amount by SM+ ranks***
- ✓ Increase max payout from 120% fix amount to 150% fix amount to continue encourage SM to run for 115% target achievement
- ✓ Add activities requirement for compensation payout subject to Local approval
