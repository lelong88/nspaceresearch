## Payout Breakdown by Segmentation/ Rank/ Category 📋

- Leader compensation remains less attractive than personal production. Even among the top 1% of UM+ leaders (MBA Pro Platinum); on average their income from leader role contributed only ~43% of their total income; indicating that current compensation do not sufficiently reward team development efforts.
- At the same time in 2026, UM+ income will also be impacted by discontinuation of a monthly bonus campaign launched in Oct'24 and reduction in agent's commission rate following Jul'24 regulation change (maximum FYC rate is reduced from 40% to 30%) – which will lower FYC-linked bonuses accordingly (overrides & monthly team activation bonus)
- Without adjustment, leader motivation to recruit and develop agents is increasingly constrained and may further weaken recruitment focus.
- Within the UM compensation structure, overrides (which is basic production variable bonus) accounted for significant portion of UM+ incomes.
