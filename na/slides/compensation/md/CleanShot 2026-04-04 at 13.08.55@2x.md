# Executive Summary

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.08.55@2x.png)

## Change Objective *🏆 icon*

- **Current challenges/state:**
  - UM+ compensation lacking attractiveness, Leaders prioritize personal sales over team development resulting in low recruitment and team development focus impacting manpower for future growth
  - SM compensation dominated by fixed monthly allowances of which flat bonus structure reduces motivation beyond bonus tier thresholds.

- **Change objectives:**
  (1) Enhance UM compensation to make it more attractive; focus on Top leaders MBA Pro to drive for recruitment and new agents development efforts
  (2) Enhance PSM+ fixed allowance to be more variable to encourage continuous FYP target achievement,
  (3) Continue to strengthen persistency modifier

- **Cost & risk impact:** the proposal will bring an incremental cost of around 1.1% FYP which will be offset by lower expenses on campaigns & others benefits to stay within allowable PREs 58% FYP; CAR will be increase from 3.0% (average productivity) to 10% (Top Tier); however it is operationally challenging to constructs the highest scenario and the increase in CAR is primarily driven by higher investment in Top Leaders MBA - a group of ~200 leaders with strong track-record (long tenure; high Persistency 13); the absolute amount of arbitrage gain is too modest to encourage the behavior; hence the risk could be mitigated by regular monitoring and controls.

## Change Direction *🏢 icon*

**P13** *👤 icon*
➤ Increase P13 requirement for P13 modifiers to AG/UM/SM compensations and strengthen penalty for P13 under threshold

**Producer Comp** *👥 icon*
➤ Remain unchanged with few minor adjustments & simplification to align with recent sales behaviors

**UM+ Comp** *📋 icon*
➤ Increase bonus rate of New Agent Development bonus; Team Activation bonus and Retention bonus for Top leaders - MBA Pro
➤ Simplify MBA Pro title assessment mechanism (shift from quarterly 12-month rolling production to annual production)
➤ Add new activity requirement for compensation payout subject to local approval

**PSM+ Comp** *👥 icon*
➤ Break PSM+ income comfort trap by replacing uniform tier-based modifier with modifier proportional to target achievement; encouraging continuous target achievement
➤ Add new activity requirement for compensation payout subject to local approval
