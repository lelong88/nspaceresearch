# Agent Compensation | Referral Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.33@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes *📋 icon*

- Adjustment in P13 modifier (apply from Q2/2026)
- Extend active new recruit M0 requirement to M0+M1, providing referrers with greater motivation and flexibility to recruit since most of new agents got licensed towards the end of month due to exams schedule.
- Extend to apply bonus for rehire agent ≥ 24 months

## Current Scheme

**8) Recruitment Referral Bonus:** Applied for recruiter

Referral bonus = Bonus rate \* Current month FYC of Recruiter \* P13 modifier \* DC modifier

| # monthly new agents active | Bonus rate |
|---|---|
| ≥ 1 New Agent Active (\*) recruited in current month | 20% |

- *Maximum bonus: 3 mil/ month*
- Recruiters (FA/UM+) also need to active with 1CC & 4mn FYC in the month
- (\*) New Agent Active = Agents recruited in current month and produce at least 1CC & 4mil FYC in same month M0);
- Not include re-active agents & re-hired agent

## Proposed Scheme

**8) Recruitment Referral Bonus:** Applied for recruiter

Referral bonus = Bonus rate \* Current month FYC of Recruiter \* **P13 modifier** \* DC modifier

| # monthly new agents active | Bonus rate |
|---|---|
| ≥ 1 New Agent Active (\*) recruited in current month | 20% |

- *Maximum bonus: 3 mil/ month*
- Recruiters (FA/UM+) also need to active with 1CC & 4mn FYC in the month
- (\*) New Agent Active = Agents recruited and produce at least 1CC & 4mn FYC **in FFM**;
- Not include re-active agents & re-hired agent **<24 months from previous termination**
