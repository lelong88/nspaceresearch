# PSM+ Compensation | Retention Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.12.19@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📋

- Change P13 threshold from 65% to 70%, both for bonus qualification and P13 requirement in payment year

---

*Two side-by-side panels connected by a green arrow pointing from left to right*

## Current Scheme

| Group | Bonus = Bonus rate * [Monthly + Quarterly Production Bonus in the year] |
|-------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Direct location (sales location they directly manage) | 75% of [Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those direct location] |
| Indirect location (sales location within their downline hierarchy) | 25% of [Total Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those indirect locations] |

- Maximum 12 months of fix allowance (by rank at year-end)
- *Eligibility:* At year-end, PSM+ need to achieve either:
    1. PSM+: ≥ 85% FYP target achievement AND P13 ≥ 65%. OR
    2. TOP 15 SM/PSM/SSM/PSSM based on FYP direct location production in the year AND P13 direct location ≥ 65%
- *Payment plan:* paid in 3 times in next 3 years (30% - 30% - 40%) with criteria P13 ≥65% and PSM+ still working (exclude termination because of company's decision/ DC violation)

## Proposed Scheme

| Group | Bonus = Bonus rate * [Monthly + Quarterly Production Bonus in the year] |
|-------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Direct location (sales location they directly manage) | 75% of [Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those direct location] |
| Indirect location (sales location within their downline hierarchy) | 25% of [Total Monthly + Quarterly Bonus received on those indirect locations] |

- Maximum 12 months of fix allowance (by rank at year-end)
- *Eligibility:* At year-end, PSM+ need to achieve either:
    1. PSM+: ≥ 85% FYP target achievement AND **P13 ≥ 70%**, OR
    2. TOP 15 SM/PSM/SSM/PSSM based on FYP direct location production in the year AND P13 direct location ≥ **70%**
- *Payment plan:* paid in 3 times in next 3 years (30% - 30% - 40%) with criteria P13 **≥70%** and PSM+ still working (exclude termination because of company's decision/ DC violation). Also apply new threshold to payments of Y3 payment of 2024 bonus; Y2&Y3 payment of 2025 bonus.
