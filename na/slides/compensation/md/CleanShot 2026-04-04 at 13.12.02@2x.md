# UM+ Compensation | MBA Pro Retention Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.12.02@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📋

- Additional bonus tier applicable for MBA Pro Silver
- Add minimum active requirement per month to earn bonus credits
- Increase P13 requirement in payment year from 65% to 70%

---

## Current Scheme

Retention Bonus = Bonus rate * [Total Monthly Activation Bonus & New Agent Development bonus earned in Quarter) ]

| MBA Pro title | Bonus rate |
|---|---|
| Gold | 100% |
| Platinum | 125% |

- *Eligibility:* MBA Pro Leaders qualifiers, Gold & Platinum
- *Payment plan:* Bonus is credited base on income earned for each quarter that leader have MBA Pro title. Total bonus credits accumulated during the year will be paid in 3 times in next 3 years (30% - 30% - 40%) if Maintain UM+/ PSM+ and with P13 Direct reporting ≥65% (wave P13 requirement if no P13; if promoted to PSM+ then use on PSM+ P13)

*→ Green arrow pointing to Proposed Scheme*

## Proposed Scheme

Retention Bonus = Bonus rate * [Total Monthly Activation Bonus & New Agent Development bonus earned in production year(*)]

| MBA Segment | Min requirement/month | Bonus rate |
|---|---|---|
| MBA Pro SILVER | | 80% |
| MBA Pro GOLD | ≥ 2 agent active | 100% |
| MBA Pro PLATINUM | | 125% |

- *Eligibility:* all MBA Pro Leaders Title of current year & Early bird MBA Pro for next year, including Silver & Gold & Platinum.
- *Payment plan:* bonus is credited base on income earned in production year. Total bonus credits accumulated during the year will be paid in 3 times in next 3 years (30% - 30% - 40%) if Maintain UM+/ PSM+ and P13 Direct reporting ≥70% (wave P13 requirement if no P13 yet; if promoted to PSM+ then use PSM+ P13).
- Apply new P13 ≥70% threshold to payments of Y3 payment of 2024 bonus; Y2&Y3 payment of 2025 bonus.
- *Active Agent:* Agent with 1CC & 4mil FYC/month.
- *(\*) Production Year:* The calendar year during which the UM's production is measured and qualified for MBA title
- Transition in 2026 due to changed in MBA Pro assessment mechanism:
  - *Eligible for: MBA Pro end of Dec'25 – effective in Q1/2026 (~ 170 MBA Pro).*
  - *In case bonus amount under old scheme > new scheme, calculate and payout the different amount*
