# UM+ Compensation | MBA Pro title assessment

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.39@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📝

- Assessment Frequency: Shift from **Quarterly** to Annual assessment but require minimum threshold to eligible for MBA Pro's benefits.
- This change simplify the assessment mechanism and allows UM+ to plan for achieving title easier while production requirement remains mostly the same
- Criteria is converted from New Manulife Pro to Qualified New agents, but production requirement remains similar; increase FYC requirement for Gold & Silver by 20%

---
