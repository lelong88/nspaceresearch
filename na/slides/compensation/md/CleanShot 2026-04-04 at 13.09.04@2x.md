## Market Positioning/ SWOT Analysis *🏆 icon*

- Our compensation scheme is competitive in agent rewards, particularly in top-tier performance and recruitment benefits. However, it places limited emphasis on developing UM+ roles.
- While we offer substantial retention bonuses for both agents and managers, the delayed payout structure is designed to minimize risk exposure.
- In this proposal, we will accelerate reward for manager with payout structure more skewed towards Top UM+ (MBA Pro) to make UM role attractive to agency force

*The slide features a positioning diagram with two axes: vertical axis labeled "Producer Benefits" (left side) and horizontal axis labeled "Managerial Benefits" (bottom). Four competitors are positioned as bubbles across this space, from top-left (high producer benefits, low managerial benefits) to bottom-right (low producer benefits, high managerial benefits):*

---

### Manulife *(green oval, top-left position)*

**Key Features in Comp:**

- **Comparatively low override for UM+**
- **Strong investment for quality new agent:** pay high bonus for qualified Silver Fastrack new agents and their managers/recruiters
- **Progressive production bonus for agent:** max 70% FYC (monthly + Quarterly) with fix amount bonus 1.5mil FYP/month upto 20mil/month
- **Specific reward for MDRT+** max 100 mil
- **Retention bonus available across hierarchy** but more challenging requirement for managers

---

### AIA *(beige oval, center-left position)*

**Key Features in Comp:**

- **Balanced producer vs. manager comp**
- **Minimal investment for new agent themselves** (0.5mil in first 72h & 3mil training trip) but **pay for recruiters up to first 12 months** (20% FYC of new agents)
- **Progressive production bonus for agent:** max 60% FYC + 50% SYC
- **Agent retention bonus available with wider range of bonus rate**

---

### Daiichi *(beige oval, center-right position)*

**Key Features in Comp:**

- **Rich leader OR but with high validation requirement;** specific bonus for new leaders
- **Minimal investment for new agent in compensation** (1mil/mth for M0-M6)
- **Moderate agent production bonus:** max 2mil/month + up to 32%FYC (#active month requirement)
- **Specific reward for MDRT+:** max 45mil
- Have renewal benefits 2.5% SYC for new agents

---

### Prudential *(green oval, far-right position)*

**Key Features in Comp:**

- **Rich leader OR;** specific bonus for new leaders (First 6 months); strong emphasis M0-M1 new agent active (new agent development bonus in first 12 months of agents)
- **Rich bonus for top producers:** max 80% FYC + max 9mil/mth for active months (15mil FYP) + 15%SYC
- **Specific reward for MDRT** 50mil + repeated MDRT - 10% SYC/ 10%-20%TYC
