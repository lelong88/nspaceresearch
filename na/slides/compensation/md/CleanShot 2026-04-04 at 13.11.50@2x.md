# UM+ Compensation | **Monthly Team Activation Bonus**

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.50@2x.png)

## **Summary of Changes** 📋

- Adjust # active agents requirement to reflect current agency structure & increase bonus rate to enhance UM focus on activating agents.
- Must >=2 active/month to qualify for higher MBA Pro bonus rate – act as a form of "MOC" for MBA benefit
- Include SA active
- Adjustment in P13 modifier (apply from Q2/2026)

---

## **Current Scheme**

Monthly Team Activation Bonus = (A.1) * P13 modifier

| # Active agents (*) / managers in direct report level 1 | A.1- Bonus (*) (% total M&Q bonus of direct report level 1) | |
|---|---|---|
| | **Non- MBA Leader** | **MBA Leader** |
| ≥ 7 | 55% | 70% |
| 5 – 6 | 45% | 60% |
| 3 - 4 | 35% | 50% |
| 1 – 2 | 25% | 40% |

| % Actual P13 | % P13 modifier |
|---|---|
| < 50% | x actual P13 |
| ≥ 50% and < 55% | 75% |
| ≥ 55% and < 60% | 90% |
| ≥ 60% and < 65% | 100% |
| ≥ 65% and < 70% | 105% |
| ≥ 70% | 110% |
| ≥ 75% | 115% |
| no P13 yet | 100% |

- (*) Active Agent: FYC ≥ 4mil and CC ≥ 1
- Direct report is included all FA and UM+ at level 1
- Attendance ratio ≥ 80%

| ≥ 80% | 110% |

- (*) Active Agent: FYC ≥ 4mil and CC ≥ 1, include SA active
- Direct report is included all FA and UM+ at level 1
