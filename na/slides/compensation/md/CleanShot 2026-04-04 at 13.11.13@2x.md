# Agent Compensation | New Agent Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.13@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes. *📋 icon*

- Adjustment in P13 modifier (apply from Q2/2026), and extend to apply bonus for rehired agent ≥ 24 months

---

## Current Scheme

### 1) New Agent M1-M2 Bonus:

New Agent M1-M2 bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Month | M1 (M0+1) | M2 |
|---|---|---|
| **NAB bonus** | **VND 1.5m** | **VND 1.5m** |
| Production Requirement (PR) | 1 CC & 10mil FYP | 1 CC & 10mil FYP |
| Activity Requirement (AR) | Attendant: 80%+ Meet training requirement | Attendant: 80%+ Meet training requirement |
| % Bonus received if meet | PR+AR = 100% of NAB Bonus | PR only = 50% of NAB Bonus |

- Not apply for rehired agents and re-active agents
- Claw back if new agents terminated within 12 months working

### 2) Manulife Pro Fast-track bonus

Manulife Pro Fast-track bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Requirement from M0 to M2 | Bonus (mil VND) |
|---|---|
| 3 CC & 40 mil FYP | 5 mil |
| Complete M0-M2 training & activity requirement | |

- Not apply for rehired agents and re-active agents
- Claw back if new agents terminated within 12 months working

## Proposed Scheme

### 1) New Agent M1-M2 Bonus:

New Agent M1-M2 bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier *(shown in blue text)*

| Month | M1 (M0+1) | M2 |
|---|---|---|
| **NAB bonus** | **VND 1.5m** | **VND 1.5m** |
| Production Requirement (PR) | 1 CC & 10mil FYP | 1 CC & 10mil FYP |
| Activity Requirement (AR) | Meet activity & training requirement of M0-M2 (subject to local agent development roadmap & CAO approval) | |
| % Bonus received if meet | PR+AR = 100% of NAB Bonus | PR only = 50% of NAB Bonus |

- Not apply for re-active agents & rehired agents **<24 months from previous termination**
- Claw back if new agents terminated within 12 months working

### 2) Manulife Pro Fast-track bonus:

Manulife Pro Fast-track bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier *(shown in blue text)*

| Requirement from M0 to M2 | Bonus (mil VND) |
|---|---|
| 3 CC & 40 mil FYP | 5 mil |
| Meet activity & training requirement of M0-M2 (subject to local agent development roadmap & CAO approval) | |

- Not apply for re-active agents & rehired agents **<24 months from previous termination**
- Claw back if new agents terminated within 12 months working
