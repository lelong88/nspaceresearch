# Risk Assessment and Mitigation

| Risk type | Description | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Compensation Arbitrage Risk | Risk of seller construct a dummy policy to take advantage of high compensation payment, leading to below-requirement persistency | Medium | High | The normal process is in place to control arbitrage risk as below: **Validation:** • Require **13-month persistency** (P13) across bonus and agency hierarchy. P13 requirement for bonus modifiers has been increased under new scheme. • Conduct **Post-freelook assessment** when calculating bonuses for top producers/ leaders (including Manulife Pro, MDRT, MBA) • For bonuses related to new hires (NAB, Referral/ recruitment bonus), **re-hire agents <24 months** since their previous termination **is not qualified** to be counted as new agents • Use net FYP so that early cancellations in premium holidays are not be counted • For not-taken policies, commissions and bonuses (including case count) related to those policies will be deducted from the total compensation of the current month and following months. Any policy change, including a decrease in face amount, during free look period is considered as not-taken. • For lapsed/ surrendered policies: Commissions will also be deducted if a non-annual pay mode policies lapsed or surrender within the first policy year. **Claw back:** • Clawback clause for early agent and policy termination is included in their agent contract to mitigate the potential financial impact from the advanced compensation. • Apply clawback in New Agent Bonus if agent is terminated within the first 12 months • Apply clawback for industrial hires under GTF UM+ & MAC if they leave company early • If an agent is terminated for any reason, any outstanding debt will be collected. Failures to repay debts exceeding more than 3 mil VND will result in being blacklisted. In reality, the number of agents being blacklisted is not small. **Cap on maximum exposure:** • Apply a maximum cap or fixed bonus amount to most producers' bonuses (New Agent Bonus, Referral/ recruitment bonus, MDRT bonus, Manulife Pro allowance, Agent Retention Bonus), UM new development bonus, SM fix subsidy & SM retention to limit the maximum risk exposure. **Withholding mechanism:** • **Pay commission after premium holiday period** for big cases • **For high amount bonus (retention)** for top producers/ leaders, the payment is **deferred for 3 years**. These delays in payment will further discourage exploitation of the scheme. • Additionally, **not all the bonuses are paid immediately,** payment schedule ranges from quarterly to annually. Bonuses paid monthly are subject to delayed payment for post-freelook assessment or clawback condition applied. |
