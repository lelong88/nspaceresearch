## Summary of Changes 📋

- P13 Persistency is applied across agent bonus since Apr'2024 and on average the P13 is improved across agency channel. Increase P13 requirement for P13 modifiers to reinforce enhanced sales quality

- Communicate new P13 modifiers to agency force in Q1'2026 and **apply from Q2'26**
