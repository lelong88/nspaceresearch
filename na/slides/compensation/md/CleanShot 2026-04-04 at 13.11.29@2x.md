# Agent Compensation | M-Star Bonus

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.11.29@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📝

- Change FYP requirement to align with MDRT requirement while bonus amount remains unchanged.
- Adjustment in P13 modifier (apply from Q2/2026).
- Proposal as percentage of MDRT requirement with room for local LBU to rounding the absolute amount and room to change the percentage +/-5%; absolute FYP threshold is reference based on this year MDRT2027 title requirement only.

---

## Current Scheme

### 7) a) Quarterly

M-Star Quarterly bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Quarter | YTD FYP requirement | Bonus (mil VND) | |
|---------|---------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| | | **90% require** | **100% require** |
| Q1 | 125 mil | 5 | 8 |
| Q2 | 390 mil | 8 | 12 |
| Q3 | 650 mil | 10 | 15 |
| Q4 | 933.6046 mil | | 20 |

- *Mandatory requirement:* active each month in quarter (1CC & 4mil FYC)
- Claw back if agent stop working before 01 March next year

### b) Year-end catch up

M-Star Year-end catch up = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier – Total M-star Quarterly received

| FYP FY 2025 | Bonus (mil VND) |
|-------------|-----------------|
| 933.6046 mil | 55 mil |
| 1.8 bil | 75 mil |
| 2.4 bil | 100 mil |

## Proposed Scheme

### 7) a) Quarterly

M-Star Quarterly bonus = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier

| Quarter | YTD FYP requirement (as % MDRT requirement) | Bonus (mil VND) | |
|---------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| | | **90% require** | **100% require** |
| Q1 | **15%** ~ 160 mil for 2026 | 5 | 8 |
| Q2 | **40%** ~ 430 mil for 2026 | 8 | 12 |
| Q3 | **70%** ~ 750 mil for 2026 | 10 | 15 |
| Q4 | **100%** ~1,073.6452 mil for 2026 | | 20 |

*A green arrow icon indicates the transition from the Current Scheme to the Proposed Scheme.*

- *Mandatory requirement:* active each month in quarter (1CC & 4mil FYC)
- Claw back if agent stop working before 01 March next year

### b) Year-end catch up

M-Star Year-end catch up = Bonus * P13 modifier * DC modifier – Total M-star Quarterly received

| FYP FY 2026 | Bonus (mil VND) |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **100% MDRT** -1,073.6452 mil for 2026 | 55 mil |
| **200% MDRT** ~ 2.0 bil for 2026 | 75 mil |
| **300% MDRT** ~3.0 bil for 2026 | 100 mil |

(\*) Bonus threshold is set as percentage of MDRT requirement which can be change under local approval by +/-5%
