## Process Flow

### Phase 1: Interview *(similar GTF SM+)*

| Responsible Party | Process Step |
|---|---|
| Recruiter _ RH | **1st layer Interview & Review Application** |
| Agency Operation (AO) | Review application, Due diligence |
| AO, MBA training | Official Interview with Manulife — *(Stop if Fail)* |
| CAO, Head ASBD, Head MBA, Head Line1B | MAC committee interview — *(Stop if Fail)* |

**Selection criteria:**
- *FYP L12M*
- *#CA L6M*
- *$income L12M*
- *P13*
- *# working experience in LI*
- *Education, Age*
- *CIC credit check* *(in red)*
- *Reference check* *(in red)*

---

### Phase 2: On Boarding *(SM GTF MAC)*

| Responsible Party | Process Step |
|---|---|
| AO | Issuing Offer Letter, Support MIT, Contract sign |
| CRE & AMkt | Review -> Approval/Reject of office location provided by GTF SM — *(Only move to next step if only location approved)* |
| SM GTF MAC | Ensure construction implementation procedures comply with legal regulations and Manulife's requirement |
| CRE & AMkt | Sign Project Acceptance if meet requirement — *(requirement: leasing contract, construction contract, invoice, actual look n feel align with guideline)* |
