# MAC scheme | 2c. Catchup on Guarantee monthly office subsidy in first 3 years

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.18.07@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📝

- Adjustment in P13 modifier to align with 2026 compensation.
- Different tiers for catchup amount depends on office size and office location (whether office is in the urban cities or in provinces).

---

## Pilot Scheme:

**c) Catch-up on guarantee monthly office subsidy in first 3 years:**

= [Catchup amount x P13 modifier x Office quality modifier] – [∑Actual received (2a+2b)]

*Condition:* achieve accumulative ∑1mAA target by checkpoint & qualify all activities required each month

**Catchup amount:**

| Checkpoint | M6 | Every 3 months from M9 until M24 | Every 3 months from M25 until M36 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Based on ∑ 1mAA in | L6M | L3M | L3M |
| Catchup amount (VND) | **240 mil** *(6 x 40mil)* | **120 mil** *(3 x 40mil)* | **90 mil** *(3 x 30mil)* |

*Note: For internal MAC, 1mAA is only based on production of newly recruited agents/leaders from MAC contracted month onwards and exclude rehire <24mths*

## Proposed Scheme:

**c) Catch-up on guarantee monthly office subsidy in first 3 years:**

= **[Catchup amount x P13 modifier x Office quality modifier]** – [Actual received **(2a+2b)**]

*Condition:* achieve accumulative ∑1mAA target by checkpoint & qualify all activities required each month

| Checkpoint | | M6 | Every 3 months from M9 until M24 | Every 3 months from M25 until M36 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
