# Manulife Agency Center (MAC) | 3. Bonus for direct manager to recruit/develop MAC

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.18.11@2x.png)

## Summary of Changes 📝

- Adjustment in P13 modifier to align with 2026 compensation.
- Different tiers for catchup amount depends on office size and office location (whether office is in the urban cities or in provinces).

## Pilot Scheme:

### a) Recruitment bonus:

**One-off 50 mil per MAC recruited successfully**

- *Eligibility:* SM+ who is the direct manager of GTF MAC and directly recruited GTF MAC. Not applicable to internal MACs.
- MAC contract must remain valid through M3 and MACD must still be working at PSM+ level at M3

### b) Monthly MAC development bonus:

**Monthly MAC development bonus = 3% x Monthly actual FYP of MAC for first 12 months**

- *Eligibility:* SM+ who is the direct manager of GTF/Internal MAC and directly recruited GTF/Internal MAC.
- MAC must achieve at least 60% of their monthly FYP target
- Monthly payment, maximum total bonus during first 12 months of MAC is 100mil/ MAC
- MAC contract must remain valid through M3 and MACD must still be working at PSM+ level at M3
- For internal MAC, FYP is only based on production of newly recruited agents/leaders from MAC contracted month onwards and exclude rehire <24mths

## Proposed Scheme:

### a) Recruitment bonus:

**One-off <span style="color:green">50 mil</span> per MAC recruited successfully**

- *Eligibility:* SM+ who is the direct manager of GTF MAC and directly recruited GTF MAC. Not applicable to internal MACs.
- MAC contract must remain valid through M3 and MACD must still be working at PSM+ level at M3

### b) Monthly MAC development bonus:

**Monthly MAC development bonus = <span style="color:green">3%</span> x Monthly actual <span style="color:green">FYP</span> of MAC for first 12 months**

- *Eligibility:* SM+ who is the direct manager of GTF/Internal MAC and directly recruited GTF/Internal MAC. *Not applicable to internal MACs due to closed office.* *(highlighted in yellow)*
- MAC must achieve at least **60%** of their monthly FYP target
- Monthly payment, maximum total bonus during first 12 months of MAC is **100mil/ MAC**
- MAC contract must remain valid through **M12** and MACD must still be working at PSM+ level at **M12 and directly report to recruiter till M12** *(highlighted in yellow)*
- For internal MAC, FYP is only based on production of newly recruited agents/leaders from MAC contracted month onwards and exclude rehire <24mths
