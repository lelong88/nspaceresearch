# MAC scheme | 1. One-off setup subsidy

![Source](../images/CleanShot 2026-04-04 at 13.17.10@2x.png)

## **Summary of Changes** 📋

- Increase one-off setup subsidy both for signage and set-up costs based on latest actual costs occurred in recent pilot MACs
- Different tiers for this subsidy depends on office size and office location (whether office is in the urban cities or in provinces). Subsequently, advance amount is variable 1/3 of total set-up cost, instead of fix 100 mil.

---

### **Pilot Scheme: Same for both GTF and internal SM/SSM**

- Company sponsor 100% Signage costs (110mil) + maximum 300mil VND for set-up costs
- Advance 100mil when sign MAC contract and remainder are reimbursed after construction office acceptance (this is based on vendor's estimation for setting up office following our design & standards).
- Estimated set-up costs (base on latest design):

| Signage | Maximum office set-up |
|---------|----------------------|
| 110 mil | 70m2: 300 mil |

*→ Green arrow pointing right →*

### **Proposed Scheme: Vary per office size & location (urban/province)**

- Company sponsor 100% Signage costs + maximum office set-up costs per office size and location
- Advance 1/3 of office set-up when sign MAC contract and remainder are reimbursed after construction office acceptance (this is based on vendor's estimation for setting up office following our design & standards).
- Estimated set-up costs (base on latest design):

| MAC | Signage | Maximum office set-up | |
|-----|---------|----------------------|------|
| | | **Provinces** | **Urban cities** |
| Small size (~70m2) | **130** mil | **300** mil | **400** mil |
| Big size (~150m2) | **160** mil | **500** mil | **600** mil |

*Note:* One-off setup subsidy for big size is only applicable for DRD+
