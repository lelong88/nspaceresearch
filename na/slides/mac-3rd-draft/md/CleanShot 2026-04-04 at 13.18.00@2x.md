---

**Pilot Scheme:**

**a) Monthly office subsidy:**

Monthly office subsidy = **8%** x Monthly actual **FYP** x **P13** modifier x **Office quality** modifier

*Note:* For internal MAC

- First 3 years, monthly FYP is only based on production of newly recruited agents/leaders from MAC contracted month onwards and exclude rehire <24mths
- From year 4 (M37+), monthly FYP includes all agents/leaders
