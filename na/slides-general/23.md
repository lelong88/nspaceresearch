# Manulife Agency Center (MAC) | Existing mechanism for GTF SM+ sign-on

![Source](../images/23.jpg)

## Sign-on Bonus Eligibility Criteria

- Must have been with existing Insurer for a period of no less than 12 months; and should not have held any job for <6 months in the past 3yrs without valid reasons.
- Must have total income over VND1bn within the last 12 months.
- Avg sales performance over last 12 months must be at minimum 2x Standard FYP Target
- Avg portfolio persistency must be either (must show proof)
  - 13M Persistency Rate > 75% (preferred)
  - 19M Persistency Rate > 80%

## Bonus Amount

| Grading | Amount (capped at 1x last 12 months total income of candidate) | Approval Limit |
|---------|---------------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| SM, SSM | Up to VND 1.5 billion | CAO & CFO |
| DRD, RD, SRD | Up to VND 2.5 billion | CFO & CEO |
| AVP, SAVP, VP | Up to VND 4.5 billion | Regional Approval |

- *The final amount shall be based on EITHER (but not both):*
  - *1. Debt notification letter of prior Insurer*
  - *2. Loss of earned income from prior Insurer*

## Clearance and Claw-back

| Year | Bonus Amount Cleared |
|------|---------------------|
| 1 | 40% |
| 2 | 30% |
| 3 | 30% |

- *Must achieve Standard Annual FYP Target of the year; AND*
- *Must have 19M persistency over 75% (starting in Y2).*
- *If prior year clearance criteria is not met, GTF member will have a chance to catch up in the following year as long as cumulative criteria are met. If current year is met but cumulative not met, then only current year amount is cleared.*

## Remaining Uncleared Amounts

- If after the 3 year GTF program period, or if the GTF member is removed from the GTF program for any reasons whatsoever;
- For any remaining uncleared Bonus Amount, **deduct up to 10% of monthly income** from GTF member until the uncleared Bonus Amount is paid off.

## Upon Leaving Manulife

- If for any reason the GTF member leaves Manulife (whether voluntary or involuntary), the claw-back mechanism will be activated.
- Any uncleared Bonus Amount will be clawed back by Manulife at 100%.
