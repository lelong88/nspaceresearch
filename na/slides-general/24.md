# Claw-back logic for GTF Sign-on Bonus

![Source](../images/24.jpg)

## DEDUCTION MECHANISM
**Deduction starts from Y4**

*Timeline diagram showing progression from Y1 through Y4 with decision points and consequences at each stage:*

**Y1** (Month 12) → **Y2** (Month 18) → (Month 24) → **Y3** (Month 36) → **Y4** (From month 37)

### Y1 (Month 12)
- **(1) Hit Y1 APE: Yes**
- **(2) Hit Y1 APE: No**

**Consequences:**
- (1) Record APE target hitting
- (2) No Y1 advance clearance

### Y2 (Month 18)
- **(3) Persistency >= 75%: Yes**

**Consequences:**
- (1) + (3): Clear 40% of advance

### Month 24
- **(4) Hit Y2 APE: Yes and Persistency >= 75%: Yes**
- **(5) Hit Y2 APE: No or Persistency >= 75%: No**

**Consequences:**
- (4) Clear 30% of advance
- (5) No Y2 advance clearance

### Y3 (Month 36)
- **(6) Hit Y3 APE: Yes and Persistency >= 75%: Yes**
- **(7) Hit Y3 APE: No or Persistency >= 75%: No**

**Consequences:**
- (6) Clear 30% of advance
- (7) No Y3 advance clearance

### Y4 Possible scenarios (From month 37)

- **(8) Advance is cleared off**

- **(9) Full or remained advance amount after Y3 becomes debt of SM with MVL Start deduction**

- **(10) SM+ stay with MVL and request to pay off the debt**

- **(11) SM+ leave MVL w/o debt**

- **(12) SM+ have debt, pay the debt and leave MVL**

- **(13) SM+ have debt, not pay debt, and leave MVL → blacklist**

**Details for scenario (9):**
- Full or remained advance amount after Y3 becomes debt of SM with MVL
- Deduct 10% monthly income until the advance is cleared off/SM pay off before leaving
- Blacklist SM who leaves MVL with debt
