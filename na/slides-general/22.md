## SM+ Role Description (Center)

Their role is to manage sales locations (non-FTE)  
Their compensation is variable to their target achievement  
We have 2 type of SM+:
- Internal organic promoted to SM (from agents to unit managers and then to sales managers)
- Inorganic recruitment from industry
