# Manulife Agency Center (MAC) | MAC Office Support Validation & Claw back

![Source](../images/9.jpg)

- MAC Office support is subjected to scheme maintenance validation and claw back mechanism within 3 years of scheme.
- When MAC fail their target but still maintain a certain minimum performance, MAC contract will still be maintained but we'll stop guarantee office subsidy
- When MAC fail their performance below the minimum performance or fail office quality, we'll terminate MAC contract, stop all subsidy and claw back partially our initial investment
- When MAC fail due to sales quality check (DC, P13) or resign, we'll also terminate MAC contract, stop all subsidy and claw back fully our initial investment and monthly office subsidy

## 4. MAC OFFICE SUPPORT VALIDATION

**Frequency:** Quarterly (Mar, Jun, Sep, Dec) and joined at least 6 months

| Event | 1- MAC Office Management Role | 2- Monthly Office Subsidy (8% FYP) | 3- Guaranteed Office subsidy first 3 years (40m/30m) |
|-------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| 1. Fail <50% FYP target for 2 consecutive quarters in first year | Maintain | Yes | Stop |
| 2. MAC director **demoted but within SM** (first MOC between M9-M12) | Maintain | Yes | Stop |
| 3. MAC director **fail SM MOC** and become UM+ | Terminated (*) | Stop | Stop |
| 4. MAC office **Quality check is Red** for 6 consecutive months | Terminated (*) | Stop | Stop |
| 5. **DC/Line 1B or P13 <50%** (for 6 consecutive months when P13 is available) | Terminated (*) | Stop | Stop |

*(*) MAC contract terminated: Company withdraw signage, they can still work in Manulife following normal compensation*

| Event | Construction costs subsidy | Monthly Office Subsidy (8% FYP) | Guaranteed Office subsidy first 3 years |
|-------|---------------------------|--------------------------------|----------------------------------------|

| Fail MAC validation due to #5 OR Resign within first 3 years | Claw back 100% | Claw back 100% | Claw back 100% |
| MAC contract end for any other reasons within first 3 years | Claw back from 50% up to 100% of MAC scheme subject to Company's decision | | |
