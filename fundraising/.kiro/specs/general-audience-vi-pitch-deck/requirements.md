# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This feature produces a Vietnamese-language pitch deck for the product **Step**, an AI-powered language learning app, to be shown at a technology exhibition to a general (non-technical, non-investor) audience. The deliverable is a `.pptx` file containing exactly 7 slides, generated by a Python script using the `python-pptx` library, following the slide-by-slide structure, tone, core message, flagship example, and design directions described in `decks/general-audience-vi/instruction.md`.

The core message to transmit is: *"Học ngoại ngữ qua những điều bạn thực sự yêu thích."* The flagship example used throughout the deck is a person learning English through the song *"Heal the World"* by Michael Jackson.

The deliverable consists of:
1. A Python generator script that produces the deck.
2. The generated `.pptx` file, committed alongside the script.

## Glossary

- **Deck**: The 7-slide Vietnamese-language pitch presentation for the product Step.
- **Generator_Script**: The Python script (using `python-pptx`) that builds the `.pptx` file.
- **Output_File**: The generated PowerPoint file at `decks/general-audience-vi/step-pitch-vi.pptx`.
- **Source_Instruction**: The file `decks/general-audience-vi/instruction.md` defining the slide-by-slide content, tone, example, and design directions.
- **Slide**: A single page within the Deck. The Deck contains exactly 7 Slides, numbered 1 through 7.
- **Speaker_Notes**: Vietnamese-language presenter notes (2-3 sentences) attached to each Slide via the `.pptx` notes slide feature.
- **Core_Message**: The sentence *"Học ngoại ngữ qua những điều bạn thực sự yêu thích."*
- **Flagship_Example**: The narrative of a learner using Step to learn English through the song *"Heal the World"* by Michael Jackson.
- **Accent_Color**: The single accent color used consistently across the Deck. Value: deep teal (hex `#0F5F5C`) unless the user specifies otherwise.
- **Neutral_Palette**: The warm neutral colors used for background and body text (background `#FAF7F2`, body text `#2B2B2B`, secondary text `#6B6B6B`).
- **Headline_Font**: The typeface used for slide titles. Value: a sans-serif that renders Vietnamese diacritics correctly (default: `Inter`, fallback: `Calibri`).
- **Body_Font**: The typeface used for slide body text. Same family as Headline_Font.
- **Slide_Size**: 16:9 widescreen, 13.333 inches wide by 7.5 inches tall.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Deck Structure and Slide Count

**User Story:** As a presenter at a technology exhibition, I want a Deck with exactly 7 slides in a fixed order, so that I can deliver the narrative in the flow defined by the Source_Instruction.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL produce an Output_File containing exactly 7 Slides.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL order Slides as follows: Slide 1 Hook, Slide 2 Why Traditional Methods Fail, Slide 3 Introducing Step, Slide 4 Heal the World Example, Slide 5 Why This Works, Slide 6 What Makes Step Different, Slide 7 Call to Action.
3. THE Output_File SHALL use a Slide_Size of 13.333 inches wide by 7.5 inches tall (16:9 widescreen).

### Requirement 2: Language and Terminology

**User Story:** As a Vietnamese-speaking audience member, I want all text in natural Vietnamese, so that I can understand and emotionally connect with the content.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL write all Slide titles, Slide body text, and Speaker_Notes in Vietnamese.
2. WHERE the product name appears, THE Generator_Script SHALL render it as the literal string `Step`.
3. WHERE the song title appears, THE Generator_Script SHALL render it as the literal string `Heal the World`.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL include the Core_Message verbatim as the tagline on Slide 3.
5. IF any text contains Vietnamese diacritics, THEN THE Generator_Script SHALL preserve them using UTF-8 encoding without substitution or stripping.

### Requirement 3: Per-Slide Content Format

**User Story:** As a reader of the Deck, I want each Slide to contain a Vietnamese title, Vietnamese body, a visual element, and Speaker_Notes, so that every Slide meets the output format from the Source_Instruction.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL give each Slide exactly one Vietnamese title.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL give each Slide a Vietnamese body section whose content matches the corresponding Slide description in the Source_Instruction.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL attach Vietnamese Speaker_Notes of 2 to 3 sentences to each Slide using the `.pptx` notes slide feature.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL include a visual element on each Slide, implemented as either a placeholder image, a shape, an icon composition, or a QR code placeholder as appropriate for that Slide.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL limit body text on each Slide to at most 4 bullet points or 60 words, whichever is smaller, to preserve minimal text and one clear idea per Slide.

### Requirement 4: Slide 1 - Hook

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 1 to open with an emotional, relatable hook, so that I immediately feel the problem rather than have it stated to me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL set the Slide 1 headline to a Vietnamese phrasing equivalent in meaning to *"Bạn đã học tiếng Anh nhiều năm — nhưng vẫn chưa thực sự 'chạm' vào nó?"*.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL include on Slide 1 a visual element representing a person listening to music with lyrics nearby, implemented as a shape, icon composition, or placeholder image.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL limit Slide 1 to the single headline and at most one supporting line of body text.

### Requirement 5: Slide 2 - Why Traditional Methods Fail

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 2 to name the real reason traditional methods fail, so that I understand why the status quo does not work.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL give Slide 2 a Vietnamese title that frames the gap between traditional learning and content the learner loves.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL limit Slide 2 body text to at most 3 bullet points.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL state on Slide 2 that traditional methods disconnect learning from content the learner loves, that typical vocabulary topics do not match real interests, and that motivation fades when learning feels like a chore.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid naming or criticizing any specific competitor product on Slide 2.

### Requirement 6: Slide 3 - Introducing Step

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 3 to introduce Step with a reframe and tagline, so that I understand the product's positioning in one slide.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL include on Slide 3 the reframe from *"Phương pháp nào để học tiếng Anh?"* to *"Nội dung nào bạn muốn sống cùng trong tiếng Anh?"*.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL display on Slide 3 the tagline **"Step — Học ngoại ngữ qua những điều bạn thực sự yêu thích."** with visual emphasis distinct from body text.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL include on Slide 3 a one-sentence Vietnamese explanation describing Step as an AI-powered app that builds the learning journey around content the user already loves, including songs, novels, manga, podcasts, and films.

### Requirement 7: Slide 4 - Heal the World Example

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 4 to walk through the Flagship_Example concretely, so that I see exactly how Step works on real content.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL frame Slide 4 around a user choosing the song `Heal the World`.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL present on Slide 4 five concrete activity types in Vietnamese: reading lyrics with contextual vocabulary, listening and pronunciation practice, cultural exploration (including the contrast between *heal* and *cure*), flashcards generated from the lyrics, and writing to restate the song's message.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL close Slide 4 with a Vietnamese sentence conveying that the learner is not only learning English but living with a song they love.

### Requirement 8: Slide 5 - Why This Works

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 5 to state the underlying insight, so that I understand why learning through beloved content is more effective.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL state on Slide 5 that every word in a language carries a worldview.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL illustrate on Slide 5 that *"heal the world"* is not just three words but a way of seeing healing as collective, emotional, and hopeful.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL include on Slide 5 the Vietnamese one-liner *"Mỗi ngôn ngữ mới là một phiên bản mới của chính bạn."* with visual emphasis distinct from body text.

### Requirement 9: Slide 6 - What Makes Step Different

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 6 to list what makes Step different using short punchy points, so that I can quickly grasp the product's differentiators.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL present on Slide 6 exactly 4 differentiator points in Vietnamese: AI adapting difficulty to the learner's level, diverse content types (songs, comics, novels, podcasts), deep connection between exercises and loved content, and absence of empty gamification (no fake scores or pressure-inducing streaks).
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render each of the 4 points on Slide 6 with an accompanying icon or shape as a visual anchor.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL keep each of the 4 points on Slide 6 at or below 16 words.

### Requirement 10: Slide 7 - Call to Action

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 7 to close with an inviting call to action and next step, so that I know how to engage with Step after the talk.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL set the Slide 7 headline to the Vietnamese invitation *"Bắt đầu với bài hát, cuốn sách, bộ phim bạn yêu nhất."*.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL include on Slide 7 the Vietnamese sub-text *"Ngôn ngữ không phải là môn học. Nó là cánh cửa vào một thế giới mới — và một phiên bản mới của chính bạn."*.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL display on Slide 7 the product name `Step`, a QR code placeholder shape, and a simple next-step line in Vietnamese covering download, sign up, or learn more.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL phrase the call to action on Slide 7 as an invitation rather than a demand.

### Requirement 11: Visual Design System

**User Story:** As an exhibition visitor, I want the Deck to feel clean, modern, and warm, so that it reads as friendly and non-corporate.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL apply the Neutral_Palette background `#FAF7F2` to every Slide.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL use the Accent_Color `#0F5F5C` as the sole accent on titles, shapes, or emphasized text, with no additional accent colors beyond Neutral_Palette and Accent_Color.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render every Slide title using the Headline_Font at a size of at least 40 points.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL render body text using the Body_Font at a size between 18 and 28 points inclusive.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL leave at least 0.5 inches of margin between any text frame and the Slide edge on all four sides.
6. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid stock-photo language-learning clichés such as depictions of a person wearing a headset in front of national flags.

### Requirement 12: Tone and Content Constraints

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want the Deck to feel accessible and emotional rather than corporate, so that the message lands with a general audience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL keep each Slide to one clear idea as defined by the Slide's section in the Source_Instruction.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid technical jargon on every Slide, using plain Vietnamese phrasing that a general audience can understand without a technical background.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid naming or criticizing any specific competitor product on every Slide.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid defensive claims (such as "we are better than X" or "unlike other apps") on every Slide.

### Requirement 13: Generator Script and Output Artifact

**User Story:** As the feature owner, I want a reproducible Python generator and a committed `.pptx` file, so that the Deck can be regenerated and shared.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL be a single Python file located at `decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py`.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL use the `python-pptx` library to build the Output_File.
3. WHEN the Generator_Script is executed from the workspace root with `python decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py`, THE Generator_Script SHALL write the Output_File to `decks/general-audience-vi/step-pitch-vi.pptx` and exit with status code 0.
4. IF the Output_File already exists when the Generator_Script runs, THEN THE Generator_Script SHALL overwrite it.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL encode all Vietnamese strings as UTF-8 string literals in source.
6. THE Generator_Script SHALL complete execution within 30 seconds on a standard developer laptop.
7. THE repository SHALL contain a generated Output_File at `decks/general-audience-vi/step-pitch-vi.pptx` after the feature is complete.
