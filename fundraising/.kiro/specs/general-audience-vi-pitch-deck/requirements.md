# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This feature produces a Vietnamese-language pitch deck for the product **Step**, an AI-powered language learning app, to be shown at a technology exhibition to a general (non-technical, non-investor) audience. The deliverable is a `.pptx` file containing exactly 8 slides (1 shared title slide + 7 minimalist content slides), generated by a Python script using the `python-pptx` library, hosted on the Cloudflare R2 bucket `step-pitch-decks` and served via the `slides.nspace.is` CDN domain.

The deck is presented by the founder **Long Lê** and follows a strict minimalist design brief: each slide is typography-forward with one focal point, no bullet points, and no feature lists. The speaker carries the narrative; the slide is the punctuation. The core message transmitted is: *"Học ngoại ngữ qua những điều bạn thực sự yêu thích."* The flagship anchor is the song *"Heal the World"* and the etymology of the word *"decision"* (Latin: "to cut off").

The deliverable consists of:
1. A Python generator script at `decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py` and its supporting `assets.py`.
2. The generated `.pptx` uploaded to R2 at `step-pitch-vi.pptx` (latest alias) + `step-pitch-vi-<timestamp>.pptx` (snapshot). **No `.pptx` is written to local disk.**

## Glossary

- **Deck**: The final 8-slide Vietnamese pitch deck (1 shared title + 7 content slides).
- **Title_Slide**: The shared title slide at `decks/title-slide-vi.pptx`, reused verbatim for every Vietnamese deck in the repo.
- **Content_Slides**: The 7 minimalist slides built by `generate_deck.py::build(prs)` and appended onto the title slide.
- **Generator_Script**: The Python script at `decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py` that assembles and uploads the deck.
- **Assets_Module**: The sibling `decks/general-audience-vi/assets.py` that handles SVG rasterization, vendored-asset loading, title-slide opening, and R2 upload.
- **R2_Bucket**: The Cloudflare R2 bucket `step-pitch-decks`.
- **Public_URL**: `https://slides.nspace.is/step-pitch-vi.pptx` (latest alias).
- **Core_Message**: The sentence *"Học ngoại ngữ qua những điều bạn thực sự yêu thích."* (appears on the shared title slide).
- **Accent_Color**: Deep teal, hex `#0F5F5C`.
- **Neutral_Palette**: Warm cream `#FAF7F2` background, body `#2B2B2B`, muted `#6B6B6B`.
- **Speaker_Notes**: 2–3 sentence Vietnamese presenter notes attached to each content slide.
- **Slide_Size**: 16:9 widescreen, 13.333 × 7.5 inches.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Deck Structure and Slide Count

**User Story:** As a presenter at a technology exhibition, I want a Deck that opens with the shared Vietnamese title slide and is followed by 7 minimalist content slides, so that the audience sees consistent branding up front and each subsequent slide is a single memorable focal point.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL produce a Deck containing exactly 8 slides (1 Title_Slide + 7 Content_Slides).
2. THE Deck SHALL open with the shared Title_Slide pulled from `decks/title-slide-vi.pptx`.
3. THE Content_Slides SHALL follow in this order: Slide 1 Hook ("Heal the World"), Slide 2 What Step Is, Slide 3 The Founder, Slide 4 The Insight ("decision"), Slide 5 Why It Matters, Slide 6 The Promise, Slide 7 Closing.
4. THE Deck SHALL preserve a Slide_Size of 13.333 × 7.5 inches (16:9 widescreen) on every slide.

### Requirement 2: Language and Terminology

**User Story:** As a Vietnamese-speaking audience member, I want every visible text element in natural Vietnamese (except the two English anchors "Heal the World" and "decision" which are the narrative device), so that I can follow the deck without translation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL write every Content_Slides text element in Vietnamese EXCEPT the anchor phrases `"Heal the World"` (Slide 1 hero) and `"decision"` (Slide 4 hero), which are rendered verbatim in English as the narrative device.
2. WHERE the product name appears, THE Generator_Script SHALL render it as the literal string `Step`.
3. WHERE the founder's name appears, THE Generator_Script SHALL render it as the literal string `Long Lê`.
4. IF any text contains Vietnamese diacritics, THEN THE Generator_Script SHALL preserve them using UTF-8 encoding.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the public URL as the literal string `https://step.is/vi` on the closing slide.

### Requirement 3: Minimalist Design Brief

**User Story:** As an exhibition visitor, I want each content slide to carry one focal point rather than a list of ideas, so that I can internalize the narrative the speaker is delivering rather than reading from the slide.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render each Content_Slide with at most 16 visible words of text (brief intent: each slide holds ONE focal point).
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL NOT render any bullet-pointed list on any Content_Slide.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render each Content_Slide with at most one `hero` phrase and at most one optional `sub` line beneath it.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL attach Vietnamese Speaker_Notes containing at least one sentence terminator to every Content_Slide.

### Requirement 4: Visual System

**User Story:** As a viewer, I want the deck to read as clean, warm, and typography-forward rather than corporate, so that it feels like an invitation rather than a pitch.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL paint every Content_Slide with the Neutral_Palette background `#FAF7F2`.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL use the Accent_Color `#0F5F5C` as the sole accent for hero phrases and decorative shapes.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render body and muted text only in the colors `#2B2B2B` and `#6B6B6B` respectively.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL avoid stock-photo clichés (e.g., headsets with flags). Visual assets come from the vendored real product assets (logo, hero image, app-store badges) or from minimal palette-only shapes.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL leave at least 0.5 inches of margin between any text frame and the slide edge on all four sides.

### Requirement 5: Slide 1 — Hook

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want the first content slide to drop me directly into the emotional anchor (a song title I know), so that I feel rather than parse the opening.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the hero string `"Heal the World"` on Slide 1 as a centered text run.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the sub-line `Học tiếng Anh qua bài hát bạn yêu.` beneath the hero.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL embed the real vendored hero image (`vendor_assets/hero-image.png`) on Slide 1.

### Requirement 6: Slide 2 — What Step Is

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want Slide 2 to name the product and its promise in as few words as possible, so that I know what Step is before any pitch.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the real Step logo (rasterized from `vendor_assets/logo.svg`) centered above the wordmark on Slide 2.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the wordmark `Step` as a centered text run on Slide 2.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the sub-line `Vừa học. Vừa dùng.` beneath the wordmark.

### Requirement 7: Slide 3 — The Founder

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want the speaker to introduce themselves with a single name-forward slide, so that the story that follows has a clear voice.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the founder name `Long Lê` as the hero on Slide 3.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the sub-line `Ngoại ngữ là một cánh cửa.` beneath the hero.

### Requirement 8: Slide 4 — The Insight ("decision")

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want the emotional center of the deck to hold a beat of silence, so that the speaker's delivery of the etymology can land.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the English word `decision` as the hero on Slide 4.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the Vietnamese gloss `"cắt bỏ"` beneath the hero (with curly quotes preserved).
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render a thin vertical accent rule between the two text elements, representing the "cut" visually.

### Requirement 9: Slide 5 — Why It Matters

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want a single-phrase slide that names why the word story matters, so that the insight from Slide 4 lands with a generalizable claim.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the hero phrase `Câu chuyện là cánh cửa.` as a single centered text run on Slide 5.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render a minimal door motif (rectangle + handle) on Slide 5 using only palette colors.

### Requirement 10: Slide 6 — The Promise

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want an honest two-line slide that resists over-promising, so that I trust what follows.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the hero phrase `Step không làm việc học dễ hơn.` on Slide 6.
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the sub-line `Step nuôi dưỡng sự tò mò của bạn.` beneath the hero.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render Slide 6 with pure typography — no additional shapes or images.

### Requirement 11: Slide 7 — Closing

**User Story:** As an audience member, I want the closing slide to leave me with a memorable quote and a concrete way to reach the product, so that I can act after the talk.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the hero quote `"Giáo dục là thắp lên một ngọn lửa."` on Slide 7 (with curly quotes preserved).
2. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the signature `Step — Long Lê` beneath the quote.
3. THE Generator_Script SHALL render the URL `https://step.is/vi` in a muted text line below the signature.
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL embed both the real App Store download badge and the real Google Play badge on Slide 7.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL render a small flame accent motif on Slide 7 as the single decorative element.

### Requirement 12: Generator Script, Assets, and Hosting

**User Story:** As the feature owner, I want a reproducible Python generator that hosts the deck on R2 and keeps no local artifact, so that the deck can be regenerated, shared via a stable URL, and never accidentally committed to the repo.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Generator_Script SHALL be a single Python file at `decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py`.
2. THE Assets_Module SHALL be a sibling Python file at `decks/general-audience-vi/assets.py` and handle all network I/O, SVG rasterization, and title-slide opening.
3. WHEN the Generator_Script is executed with `python decks/general-audience-vi/generate_deck.py`, THE Generator_Script SHALL assemble the Deck in memory and upload it to the R2_Bucket under two keys: `step-pitch-vi-<UTC-timestamp>.pptx` (snapshot) and `step-pitch-vi.pptx` (latest alias).
4. THE Generator_Script SHALL NOT write the Deck to local disk under any circumstance.
5. THE Generator_Script SHALL print the Public_URL of both uploaded keys on stdout.
6. THE Generator_Script SHALL complete execution within 30 seconds on a standard developer laptop.
7. THE Generator_Script SHALL overwrite the `step-pitch-vi.pptx` latest-alias key on every run.
